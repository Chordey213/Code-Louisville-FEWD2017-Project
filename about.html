About us

Anderson Park was started in Fern Creek in 2006. The first home was occupied in spring of 2007 and the last one was purchased in September 2013. There are 4 different floor plans with some units having attached garages and others with spacious attics and detached garages. The property is 12.5 acres and the grounds are landscaped with a variety of blooming trees and roses. In November 2013 the development was turned over to the homeowners for self-management. Anderson Park is managed by 7 elected board of directors, all of which are resident homeowners.
